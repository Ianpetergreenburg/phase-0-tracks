A Guide to Faking Cooking Competency

Many people think that being a good cook takes effort. Like the terrible Mr. Trump would interrupt with Wrong! It really doesn't take much work at all and there is no better example than the French Mother Sauces. They are the fancily named bases for many simple yet seemingly complex soups and sauces.

These are the five main sauces to know.

  1. Béchamel
  2. Velouté
  3. Espagnole
  4. Sauc Tomat
  5. Hollandaise
and sometimes mayonaise.
Chef's might insist that these sauces important to perfect. Whatever, guys. Practically anyone can make a good enough version of these to impress their friends and family.
