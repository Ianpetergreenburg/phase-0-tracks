A Guide to Faking Cooking Competency

Many people think that being a good cook takes effort. Like the terrible Mr. Trump would interrupt withWrong! It really doesn't take much work at all and there is no better example than the French Mother Sauces. They are the fancily named bases for many simple yet seemingly complex soups and sauces.

These are the five main sauces to know.

  1. Béchamel
  2. Velouté
  3. Espagnole
  4. Sauc Tomat
  5. Hollandaise
